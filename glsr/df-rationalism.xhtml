Rationalism A philosophy that places a high regard on logic and reason.
In it's strictest use, the term was used to refer to Cartesian Rationalism (also known as "Continental Rationalism") which emphasized it's regard for reason to the point of being overly suspictious of empirical observations - but these days, in the English-speaking world, it is more often used to refer to a synthesis between Rationalism and Empiricism.
For more information, you can read the article in Rational Wiki: https://rationalwiki.org/wiki/Rationalism
