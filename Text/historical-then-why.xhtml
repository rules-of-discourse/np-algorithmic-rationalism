Why Then Algorithmic Rationalism

It is for this reason that Algorithmic Rationalism is being developed for the purpose of allowing rationality to rise above the noise. It's goal is to provide a means by which to as objectively tell honest discussion apart from irrational rhetoric without having to rely on any foregone conclusions of what is or isn't true.

It is hoped that it may be possible for the skills that would be developed under the guideance of this philosophy to be spread to society at-large, and that therefore a more rational, sane, and just world may come as a result.
